Graduation

Across the stage

Memories

With our hands raised, our hearts brimming with pride,
we walked hand in hand.
Book closed, our tales unleashed,
fearless and bold into the world.

Dark nights and bright mornings
have given way to the light of the future.
Dreams soar, farewell linger,
Achapter closes with the horizon sight.

We head off, ready to create the life
that awaits us, memories in hand.
